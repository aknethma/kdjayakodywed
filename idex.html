About the Legend

Kandiah Duraiswamy Jayakody was one of the most revered figures in the history of Sri Lankan music. Born in Udugama, his artistic journey began at a young age, influenced by his father, M. Duraiswamy, a renowned musician.

His unique ability to merge classical and folk traditions, combined with his rich, emotional voice, made him a cornerstone of Sri Lankan musical heritage. His contributions span across traditional music, film soundtracks, and cultural preservation.
